Port Scanning

View More

Port scanning is a network security technique used to identify open ports and services available on a host or network. It involves sending a series of messages to the target device and analyzing the responses to determine which ports are open, closed, or filtered.

What does it mean?

View More

Port scanning is a cybersecurity technique used to identify open ports and the services running on a networked device, such as a server or router. It serves various purposes, including vulnerability assessment, network mapping, and intrusion detection.
